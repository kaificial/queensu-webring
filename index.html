About the QueensU Webring

The QueensU Webring connects student-built sites into one shared network, making it easy to discover projects, portfolios, and stories from across the Queen's community.

Each site links to the next, keeping student work visible and encouraging collaboration.

To learn more about what a webring is, click here.

How to Join

Fork the repo, add your site to the end of the sites JSON array.

Open a Pull Request with verification (e.g., LinkedIn) showing your connection to Queen's University.

Your PR will be reviewed and merged if you meet eligibility requirements and your site contains no inappropriate content.
