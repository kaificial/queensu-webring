About the QueensU Webring

The QueensU Webring connects student-built sites into one shared network, making it easy to discover projects, portfolios, and stories from across the Queen's community.

Each site links to the next, keeping student work visible and encouraging collaboration.

How to Join

Pick the webring that fits your program, then submit a pull request with the corresponding details. Once approved, your site will appear in the directory and function as part of the ring!

Have a question or want to add a new ring? Reach out to the organizers and we will help you get set up.
